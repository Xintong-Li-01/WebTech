Meet Rosemary

Hi all! My name’s Rosemary and my foster moms think I am the cleverest kitty they’ve ever met! I was lost and scared, roaming the streets of Shrewsbury MA, when I decided to run into the house of four Tufts Veterinary students! I made myself right at home with the girls, knowing I would be well cared for in having my litter of kittens with these vet students.

A few weeks later, we all found out I have epilepsy and have occasional 30 second-long seizures. But the girls fixed me right up with a delicious chicken-flavored drug called Keppra that has been keeping my seizures in check. Now having weaned and adopted out my four beautiful kittens, I am ready to find my own forever home with someone who can keep an eye on my epilepsy and give me my medication regularly! I am the sweetest, chattiest momma cat who loves her catnip mice, long snoozes on beds and basking in the sunlight. I am a 1 year old, now spayed, female tabby cat, and potentially be ok living with a low energy dog or cat. Please ask my foster mom for more information and we can arrange a time to meet!

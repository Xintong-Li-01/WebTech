Meet Jade

Jade is a beautiful 45 pound, 3.75 -year- old Lab mix. He was adopted from us as a puppy, but now needs to be rehomed due to his anxiety and the addition of a new baby in the house. Jade needs a home with active, dog-experienced owners and no young children or other dogs or cats. Jade thrives on structure and will need training, exercise, and positive reinforcement. His family says he is a great dog to have around. This handsome boy enjoys lounging in the sun, cuddling, playing, and car rides; but his favorite activity is going for walks. Jade still has puppy-like qualities, and he loves to run around his fenced yard and play fetch, though a fenced yard isn't mandatory.

Meet Cleo

Cleo is looking for the perfect home for her! She plays too roughly to live with young kids so she is looking for an adult only home or one with older kids. She is being introduced to other cats/kittens to assess her needs, as most kittens really need another kitten buddy. So far, it looks like Cleo would prefer a younger kitten buddy, so I will be working to find her a friend and ultimately adopt her out as a pair with her new buddy. This process may take a few weeks, but it's to ensure she and her new buddy are happy longterm! If you are applying for Cleo and her buddy, please indicate that you've read this and you understand her needs.

Please fill out an application to be considered and describe the personalities traits you are looking for in a companion, as all kittens will be matched into homes based on personality and their needs (not looks).

Applications can be found: www.kittennuggets.org/adopt

More pictures and videos can be found on instagram: @kitten.nuggets

The adoption donation is $125/kitten, which includes spay/neuter surgery, up to date vaccines, microchip, deworming, flea preventative, fiv/felv test

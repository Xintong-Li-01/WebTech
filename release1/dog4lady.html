Meet Lady

Lady is a young mixed breed who gave birth to her pups prior to coming up for adoption. She is the sweetest dog. Her pups, Arlo, Brenda, Carson, Dawson, Ernie, Francesca, Georgie, Harry, and Izzie were born on 12/23/20 in SC. We look for work-at-home situations for our dogs as many have been abandoned and need someone at home who can put them into a routine and provide training. We are looking for local adopters as we follow up closely with our adopters. Please be compliant with our holistic protocol so that your dog will live a long and healthy life.

Due to Covid-19 we do not have walk-in hours and we have put all out of state adoption on hold. We are currently adopting local to the Sudbury, MA/Metrowest area. Our phones cannot hold that many messages so please fill out the application and wait for the response. http://www.saveadog.org/applytoadopt.asp.

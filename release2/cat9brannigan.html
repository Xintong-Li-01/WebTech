Meet Brannigan

Cats find their way to our door by more routes than we can count. In the case of Brannigan, he hails from the city of Chelsea. He was huddled in the center of a rotary afraid to move in any direction. A kind man pulled over who owns cats and amazingly had a carrier in his car. He ran over and put the carrier down and backed away. Instantly Brannigan ran in relieved to be saved.

Time went by and no owner came forward for this beautiful quiet cat. He was already neutered but no microchip. Now he is with us and has shown us he can be a sweetie and likes being petted on his head and scratched behind the ears. He still prefers his carrier safe zone to be loose in the cat room but we think that will change in time. He doesn't mind other cats being near if they are not aggressive and give him space. A home without children under 15 would probably be best since he is not brave enough to be picked up. He is 12 lbs and estimated to be about 2 yrs.old

To apply for a cat or kitten, please fill out the adoption application from this page: http://www.medfieldshelter.com/adoption-forms/cat

If a cat is listed on our website, that means that cat is still available for adoption.

The Shelter is staffed primarily by volunteers so adoption meetings take place by appointment. Our adoption fee for adult cats is $125 if 5 years old or younger and $75 for cats 6 years and up. The adoption fee includes the first set of vaccines, dewormer, microchip, testing for FeLV and spaying/neutering.

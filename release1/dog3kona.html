Meet Kona

Kona is a 2 year old Cockapoo 25lbs, who is a sweet boy that needs lots of exercise, he loves being outside, playing and walking. He still has puppy tendencies and thinks soft toys are for the birds and will happily make it his goal to kill it and take out all the stuffing for you, proudly showing you his work. He would do well in a home with walkers and someone willing to go to continued training classes with him, as he is super smart. He needs a very savvy and experienced dog owner, who he understands is the boss, or he will take advantage. He already knows all the usual commands and he loves and thrives on structure. Kona is not a dog for a first time owner, he needs people who will be in control and expect good behavior and manners. Kona was surrendered because he was jealous of the new baby that came into the home, so although our trainer has worked with him, she has had no issue with him at all, but we will only place him in a home without children, grandchildren or ANY home where children visit. He is very scared and reactionary of children and needs a home where children will never be an option coming into the home, or visiting. He loves adults and feels safe around bigger people. He does better with female dogs but any dog he is placed with has to be able to hold their own or he will bully them. Kona is a great dog for someone who he will respect as his leader, once that is established with human and other dogs he relaxes and becomes the perfect dog.

Requirements for adoption for Kona: ABSOLUTELY NON NEGOTIABLE (please don't call to try to persuade us, we cannot):
NO CHILDREN, without exception, adult home only, over 21 years of age. No visiting children/grandchildren. Fenced in yard, a physical fence attached to the home without exception. No dog parks. No electric fences. But daily walking also important.If there is another dog in the home, females or small males no bigger than 30lbs.Someone home during the day/work from home.Experienced adopter/walker

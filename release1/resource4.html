Adopting the Right Cat For You

Congratulations on deciding to adopt a dog! You are embarking on a wonderful and rewarding relationship. Because adopting a new dog comes with a lot of change for both dog and dog parent, we’ve compiled a checklist to help make the transition as smooth as possible.

Necessary Items for Dogs:

  1. Food and water bowls
  2. Food (canned and/or dry)
  3. Collar
  4. Four to six-foot leash
  5. ID tag with your phone number
  6. Hard plastic carrier or foldable metal crate
  7. Dog bed
  8. Doggy shampoo and conditioner
  9. Nail clippers
  10. Canine toothbrush and toothpaste
  11. Brush or comb (depends on your dog’s coat length and type)
  12. Super-absorbent paper towels
  13. Sponge and scrub brush
  14. Non-toxic cleanser
  15. Enzymatic odor neutralizer
  16. Plastic poop baggies (biodegradable ones are best) or pooper scooper
  17. Absorbent house-training pads
  18. Variety of toys (a ball, rope, chew toy and puzzle toy are good starts)
  19. Variety of treats (such as small biscuits, larger rawhides, etc.)
  20. First-aid supplies
  21. Baby gate(s)

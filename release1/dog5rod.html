Meet Rod

Rod is a happy boy who is looking for his forever home. His records indicate Hound mix, but he looks like a pocket Pitbull as he's small. He is 7 months old and all puppy. He is super affectionate, just the most loving dog you will ever meet! He is a super playful boy, friendly with dogs and kids. He loves the snow! Rod is in a foster home with other dogs and he plays nice. He is learning not to jump up on kids. He never barks unless he's in the crate, but quiets down quickly. He's just a pup. Due to Covid we do not have drop-in or walk-in hours, but everything is done by appointment after we receive your application, pictures, and references. Please be ready to adopt as we are not able to hold dogs while you're away. We require the entire household to meet the dog in the visit so that we keep the virus exposure to a minimum. Available now. If you are interested, please fill out our online application at http://www.saveadog.org/applytoadopt.asp as we aren't always able to answer emails or return phone calls.

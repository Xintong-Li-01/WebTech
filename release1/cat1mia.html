Meet Mia

Mia is an adorable 8yr old independent lady who came to us when her owner could no longer care for her. She is a quiet kitty that does her own thing and would like to be the only pet in home. She LOVES a comfy bed and blankets to sleep on (or under, she's not picky!) She enjoys indulging in a good meal and at 17 pounds she comes with a medical letter for obesity. It will be extremely important and necessary to get her a vet appointment with your primary care vet to get her on a diet/exercise plan moving forward. If you are looking for a independent companion who likes attention on her terms, Mia might be the kitty for you.

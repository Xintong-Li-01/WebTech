Meet Molly

Molly is a 7 1/2 yr old sweetie who looks like a Lab mix. She is spayed, microchipped and up to date on shots. She is a skinny 50 lbs and may fill out to about 55 or so.

Molly was surrendered when her owner could no longer take care of her. Molly was diagnosed with exocrine pancreatic insufficient, EPI. Her pancreas does not create the enzymes to digest her food and provide her body with nutrients. We are happy to report that she is doing well taking pancreatic enzymes with every meal on her prescription diet.

Molly needs someone who is home often to maintain her three meals a day and bathroom breaks. Once she is at her maintenance weight, the plan is to move her back to twice a day feedings. We have found better prices on her enzymes and swapping out the canned science diet, we have reduced the costs associated to EPI., I have the monthly cost of her meds and special diet down to be between $400 - $550/month. She cannot go a day without her medication and plant-based alternatives will not work.

Who we are

Pet adoption is an organization that devoted to find caring homes for cats and dogs that are in the shelter. Our goal is to make shelters the first place potential adopters turn when looking to get a new pet, ensuring that all healthy and treatable pets find loving homes. We do this by breaking down misconceptions surrounding shelter pets and celebrating the unique bond between every shelter pet and parent.
